If you do not have an action plan

If you don’t have an action plan, don’t worry. There are lots of things you can do to take control of your career, like:

  • take a skills assessment to find out more about your skills and motivations
  • explore different job profiles or find a course and see what might be a good fit for you
  • read careers advice articles for help making career decisions

If you would still like to speak to an adviser and make an action plan, contact us for an appointment.
