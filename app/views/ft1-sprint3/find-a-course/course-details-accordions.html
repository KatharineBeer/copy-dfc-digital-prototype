Who this course is for

Discover the learning experience and opportunities you can expect from this course.

This free course will help you to enhance your skills in reading, writing and communicating with others. Whether you want to brush up on your skills, are looking at a future career or further learning or just want to be able to help your children with their homework better, this course will give you the practical skills to support you to achieve your goal. You’ll also gain a nationally recognised qualification that employers want to see on CVs by completing this course.

Entry requirements

Find out what qualifications and skills you will need for this course

You need to be aged 19 and over and will be assessed at the start of the course to determine the right level of support and learning.

What you'll learn

Understand the key features of this course

By the end of the course you’ll have learnt new skills such as understanding how to effectively communicate, using complex sentences and the art of persuasion with the written word and style etc.

How you'll learn

Explore how the course will be delivered

You’ll be supported by our professional and skilled specialist English Trainers with personalised training that will help you tackle and overcome any topic areas that you may struggle with. We use an online tool to assess and support learners to complete the course, targeting skills that need additional support. Training takes place either in the workplace, in a community venue, at one of our own centres or remotely. We have the flexibility to support you and work around your needs.

What you'll need to bring

No specific equipment.

How you'll be assessed

Find out how your will be assessed on the course.

Three components make up the assessment method: 1. Reading exam 2. Writing exam 3. Speaking, Listening & Communicating recorded conversations as part of a group including informal and formal discussion as well as a short talk.

What you can do next

Look at the further opportunities available.

Whether you want the qualification to be a springboard to help you secure employment or move into further learning such as an apprenticeship, achieving an English Level 2 qualification can really help you open doors and unlock opportunities for your future career goals.
