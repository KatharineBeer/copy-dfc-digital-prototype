Not everyone passes their exams on the first attempt, and if you didn’t get the grades you wanted this year, you still have lots of options. One possible route you could take is resitting assessments in any of your chosen subjects.

Your school or college will normally tell you straight away if you can resit an assessment, but if you’re not sure, you should contact them to find out.

Resit an A level assessment

If you want to resit an A level assessment, exam boards will offer exams in October. You should check with your school or college to see whether you can resit your chosen subject assessment.

Resit a GCSE assessment

If you want to resit a GCSE assessment, exam boards will offer exams in November and December. You should check with your school to see whether you can resit your chosen subject assessment.

Resit a BTEC assessment

Your options for resitting a BTEC assessment might be different depending on the subject. You should check with your school or college to see whether you can resit your chosen subject assessment.

How much it costs to resit an assessment

The cost of resitting an assessment is different depending on the assessment level, subject and exam board. You should contact your school to find out how much it might cost to resit your chosen subjects.

What to do if you cannot resit your assessment

It’s important to remember that there are lots of other routes you could take. You might want to start thinking about careers that you’re interested in or different study options.
